INSTALLATION

Installation takes 1 week and the chute can be installed on all standard models of ready mix trucks. We operate out of Tuscaloosa, Alabama using our US based experts for the initial installation but the long-term plan is to partner up with workshops throughout the US as needed.

TRAINING

The chute is easy to operate. There is a demo video provided and additional training is available. Please note, that no special licensing is required to operate this equipment.
